--- layout: blog published: true title: "Memphis Hacker Scouts in in town!!" ---

We must thank and acknowledge Maurrean Barger for bring this amazing program to the Memphis metroplex and all her hard work to put this together.


On Aug. 29th, kids from the ages  8 to 16 years old were blown way with Steven Aronson and Jay Couture teaching and mentoring them about all the great stuff that they will be working at Hackers Scouts .
Well, after the introduction about Hacker Scouts and a game were the kids could interact with each other by building a"marshmallows towers "and put their skills together as a team, they were introduce to "Anatomy of a Soldering Iron" and "Electricity Beams".


I am sure the kids can't wait to put their hands on in the first kit, the " Led Robot Blenker Kit " and I said that because this was the comments that they say when the mentors ask " What you want to get out of Hacker Scouts ?"
 

Responses :
To have fun
To learn something
To build something
To put things together
To have hands on experience
To program
To take parts away and build
To learn how to build
To grow building
To build a robot
To learn more about electronics

It is a beginning of an amazing discovery of knowledge and learning as our masters introduce to the kids the fist Core Value " The unrelenting pursuit of knowledge " .
