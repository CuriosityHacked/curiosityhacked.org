--- layout: blog published: true title: "Sparks #003 Open Lab #4 : Draw Bots" ---

 

This week the kids got to create their very own draw bots! 

 

The kids placed their legs and experimented with designs, some trying several things before finding a design that did what they wanted.

Or they did after playing with making the motors turn on and off a lot first!  Sure did love that buzzing sound they made.

 They loved decorating the bots with pipe cleaner arms, legs, wings, and even feet and hands! 

Adding the parts to make the motors off-centered was tricky but the kids did great! 

 

And it was fun seeing how different each draw bot made their designs!

 

This activity was a great success!  The kids had a lot of fun.
