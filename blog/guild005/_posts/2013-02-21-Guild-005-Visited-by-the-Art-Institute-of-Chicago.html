--- layout: blog published: true title: "Guild 005 Visited by the Art Institute of Chicago" ---

Hacker Scouts Guild #005 is prepping to welcome a very special guest for a 3D Hack-a-Thon:

Thingiverse Legend and Art Institute of Chicago 3D Queen Elizabeth Neely will be visiting our Hacker Scouts Guild to show off a very cool 3D scanning technique that makes use of an X-Box Kinect! 

Elizabeth will briefly discuss her work at the Art Institute of Chicago as Director of Digital Information and Access, then jump right to it by 3D scanning our scouts!

This event happens 02/25/2013 at 5pm MST at Estella's Cafe at 148 Bridge St. in Las Vegas, New Mexico.
