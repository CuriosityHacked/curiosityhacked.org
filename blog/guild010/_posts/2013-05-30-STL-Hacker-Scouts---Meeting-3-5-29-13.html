--- layout: blog published: true title: "STL Hacker Scouts - Meeting #3 5/29/13" ---

Our 3rd meeting went very well. The mentors switched up the proficiency assessment this week from multiple choice tests (which were a big YUCK!) to mini-challenges based on the criteria in Jeremy Blum's video and the subsequent curriculum that was created from its content. One of our mentors began the meeting with a review of some of the more complicated topics from last week's meeting. This seemed to help the scouts cement the information and the mentors were able to use the information presented in the group lecture later during 1-on-1 tutoring to help clear up any remaining confusion. 

The scouts then went straight to breadboarding their circuits and embarking on the challenges. We'll continue to use these hand-on mini-challenges, and any other course corrections needed, to enhance learning and make each meeting more enjoyable for everyone. 

Check out JeremyBlum.com for more of his tutorials.
