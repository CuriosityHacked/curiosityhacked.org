--- layout: blog published: true title: "Open Lab 1/6/13" --- Happy New Year! And boy do we have an amazing Hacker Scouts year lined up for you! More projects, more skill building, more fun! Our next Open Lab is this Sunday, January 6 from 2-5pm at AMT. Featured project: Mouse Trap Racers  Focusing on design and aerodynamics, build a racer out of simple parts and compete in our Hacker Scouts Open Road Derby. Learn what makes a racer faster and go farther and why! Winner is based on distance traveled and will get a prize! ($5 material fee)                 Needle Felting Part II: At the last Open lab, the kids BEGGED me to bring my needles and wool again for more needle felting. They ask, I listen. This time, kids will get the chance to learn how to create 2D scenes on flat felt pieces. ($5 material fee)               Soldering 101: This is a special focus workshop for those kids who don't know how to solder or need a refresher. Special attention will be given to technique and explanations will be given on the how and why. Free with the purchase of a kit. The Weevil Eye is a great kit for beginners at $9.   Other Activities: Assorted Soldering Kits ($9-$19 per kit)   Brush Bot Kits ($5 per bot)
