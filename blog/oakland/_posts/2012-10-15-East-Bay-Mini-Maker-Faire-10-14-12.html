--- layout: blog published: true title: "East Bay Mini Maker Faire 10/14/12" --- As usual, the East Bay Mini Maker Faire proved why it's the largest regional Maker Faire in the world. The variety of presentations, the enthusiasm of the participants and the visitors, the creativity and ingenuity- it was all infectious! Hacker Scouts participated by having a display at the Ace Monster Toys table, where we showed off some of the activities we have featured and guests could check out a Hydraulic Judo Bot, a BioSphere, and a Laser-Cut-then-Sewn Plushy in person. Kids and grownups alike also got to play the newly Halloween themed laser Shooting Gallery. There was a lot of enthusiasm and interest in what we are building at Hacker Scouts and even a request from a hackerspace in Hawaii to help them start their own Hacker Scouts program! We also saw a few familiar faces who stopped by to say hello. We are glad that HS is resonating with so many families and filling a need in our community! If you stopped by and learned about us today, welcome! We hope to see your family soon.
