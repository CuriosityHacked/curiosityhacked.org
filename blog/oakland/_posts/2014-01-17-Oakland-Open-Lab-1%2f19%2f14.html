Cigar Box Amplifier (Two Session project!)

Today's Oakland Open Lab electronics project: For those who joined us last Open Lab, bring back your project and we'll be building awesome cigar-box enclosures for our amp circuits!

For those who missed last Open Lab, we have a few kits left for building an amplifier (basically an mp3 speaker, small size and big sound). This is a bit harder than our kits because we're using a "protoboard", which means you have to know how the components connect together -- but we'll have excellent instructions along with mentoring, and anyone familiar with soldering should find it easy. $15 includes BOTH Sessions (1/5 and 1/19)
