Oakland Open Lab 4/21/13

2-5pm and Ace Monster Toys (6050 Lowell St., Oakland #214)

This is going to be a great Open Lab- inspired by all our Hacker Scout Gamers!

 

 

Featured Project: Installing Mods

Learn how to identify and install Mods for some of the most popular computer games being played right now! We know lots of kids and parents who want to know how to do this! Minecraft will be used as the primary example. This workshop is free but we suggest you bring your own laptop with your game of choice installed.

 

Laser Cut Mousepads

 

Minecraft Pillow Cubes

Make your own 6 sided Minecraft pillow cubes, featuring some of your favorite Minecraft characters with our fabulous sewing experts!

(Materials fee: $  , limited to 10 participants)

Pixels 101

Learn all about Pixels: what they are, how they are used, where we find them, and then make a pixel tapestry using what you have learned! (Free)
