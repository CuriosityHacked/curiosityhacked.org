Halloween is one of our favorite holidays! Join us for a Halloween Hack to finish or mod your costume before you trick or treat!

Sunday, October 27th, 2013 from 2-5pm in our new space! 6036 Telegraph Avenue, Oakland!

 

Featured Workshop: Theatrical Makeup

Join expert Alana Dill as she teaches a special workshop on face anatomy, face painting technique, and special effects to help you become anybody and anything!  Fantasy? Horror? No problem! If you can imagine it, Alana can help you become it! Workshop includes tutorial, face painting, take-home guide, and your own face paint kit to take home and continue your transformation!

Sessions start on the hour (limit 15 participants per session): 2pm, 3pm, 4pm. (Materials fee $15)

Reservations not required but if you want to ensure a spot in this fantastic workshop, you can email us at info@hackers-scouts.org

 

   Featured Workshop: Laser Cut Masks

Join the Crafty Avenger to make amazing masks using laser cut forms. Once kids choose their designs, they will use the sewing machine and/or hand sewing techniques to put it all together. Bring your mask up a notch by adding LEDs to make your design light up!

This is a great, quick beginner level project! (Materials fee: $5 plain/ $16 Light Up)

 

 

   Featured Workshop: Wands

Will you choose your wand, or will your wand choose you? Join an expert wand maker and create your own custom source of magic! (Materials Fee: Free! You can't put a price on magic.)

 

Open Workshop: 

Need a last minute costume? We will have a table of resources and a mentor to help you craft a last minute idea! Free!
