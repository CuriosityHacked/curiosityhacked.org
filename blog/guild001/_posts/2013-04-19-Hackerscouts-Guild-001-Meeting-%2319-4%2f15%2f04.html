What's Happening in Guild 001?

The scouts got straight to work this meeting, with the First Wave Scouts heading into their Badge Groups and the Second Wave scouts straight to work on their Hackerling Circuits.  It was a busy and productive night, the room was abuzz with the sounds of the next generation of hackers being raised!  It is an awesome sound to hear all these kids having such meaningful learning experiences!

LED Badge Group

The scouts in the LED badge group worked on constructing a lightsaber.  In the picture to the right is the lightsaber project.  The El wire will be shortened and they will add another length of pipe to hold an on/off button.  They sanded the clear PVC tube to diffuse the light.  The leader of the LED badge group, Shoshana Abrass said, "The lightsaber handle is made from PVC.  We had virtually all the parts needed to assemble his lightsaber.  He used sugru to attach the PVC pipe to the handle, and more sugru to make the handle grips.   We experimented with the EL wire to see whether one, two, or three strands would work best.  Finally we had to take apart the EL wire battery box, file down the handle and use a Dremel cutting tool to make everything fit inside the handle.  The last thing we're missing is a switch to turn it on/off from the outside.  He also had time to make a simple design in Inkscape and cut a pendant out of acrylic."

2D to 3D group

The 2D-3D badge group worked further on their design drawings and some on their Inkscape designs.  One scout made progress on his scale design but is still finishing his Inkscape step.  Another finished her scale digital design for two parts of the doll house.  She was able to cut her first prototype on the laser.  She learned the slots in her design were much too big, not just for the cardboard but for her final plywood design as well.  Next week we should see more scouts making their first cuts.

Core Value:  Positive Attitude[[{"fid":"526","view_mode":"full","type":"media","attributes":{"height":360,"width":480,"style":"font-size: 12px; width: 350px; height: 263px; float: right;","class":"media-element file-full"}}]]

Our core value Positive Attitude teaches the scouts that in order to be resourceful and productive, you must maintain a positive attitude.  Negativity can make it difficult for teams to work together, and make it more difficult to complete a project.  Garratt led the scouts in a closing activity where the scouts split up into their respective crews and talked more about having a positive attitude.  The activity was inspired by a Star Wars character's quote, Yoda, who said, "Anger, fear, aggression; the dark side of the Force are they. Easily they flow, quick to join you in a fight. If once you start down the dark path, forever will it dominate your destiny, consume you it will." This was to show that negative emotions can be damaging, and that positive thoughts could help in a crisis.  Garratt asked the groups to split up and come up with a positive response to a crisis.  When discussing what the doctor would do in a crisis, the Doctor Wolves crew replied, "He would keep everyone calm."  The Zombie Gummy Bears Crew were discussing what a positive response would be to a Minecraft crisis, like squid taking over the earth.  One of the scouts said, "Calm down and use the magic of the butter sword to blow it up."  The Fantastic Doctors Crew said, "Just be calm."
