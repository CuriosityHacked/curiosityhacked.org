CORE VALUE PERSISTENCE      [[{"fid":"603","view_mode":"preview","type":"media","attributes":{"height":180,"width":180,"style":"font-size: 12px;","class":"media-element file-preview"}}]]

The final lesson on positive attitude was persistence.  The assignment for next week is to identify some activity that the scout has given up on, and give it another go.  Scouts should write down (or draw a picture about) the activity, why they stopped, and what they did differently this time.
