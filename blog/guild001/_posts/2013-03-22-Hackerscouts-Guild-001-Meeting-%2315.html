What's Happening in Guild 001?

 

Our Second Wave Scouts Join The Guild!

This meeting marked the day when Hackerscouts Guild 001 finally is complete!  Our second wave of scouts joined us!  Garratt led the Opening Discussion with an Introduction game.  Each scout said the name of the scout before them and the superpower they wished to possess, then they shared their own name and their superpower.  Some of the superpowers were to fly, mindreading, transfiguration, and to make silence!  This game gave them a chance to get to know each other a little and serves to break the ice!  The scouts really enjoyed the game!  We weren't sure what it was going to be like with all those scouts in one room, but everything went smoothly!

First Wave Scouts Mentor New Members

Garratt paired off the new scouts each with a First Wave Scout.  Each First Wave scout gave the new scouts an overview of the Hackerling Circuit and the Badge tree.  They shared their progress on their circuit, showed them their enclosures, and leather pouches.  They shared stories, and made friends.  The new scouts were very attentive and this iintroduction to the circuit building process reallly helped them to get comfortable.

The Votes Are In!  The First Ever Hackerscouts Guild Leader Is....

The First Wave held the first ever election of the Guild Leader position, and Asst. Guild Leader.  It was very impressive to see how much the scouts prepared for their campaign and how well they understood the responsibilities.  It was great to see Seven scouts campaign for this coveted position. Most ran on the "responsible, on time, and good behavior" ticket.  Some just talked about how awesome they were!  And they were ALL awesome!  All the scouts had multiple votes and it was very close.  The Guild Leader position went to Skyler and Assistant Leader was a tie with Jamel and Miranda.  They will equally share the responsibilities.  The Hackerscouts Guild Leader is an important role and carries a lot of responsibility.  They must be on time for meetings, call meetings to order, run the opening and closing, and meet with adult leaders to plan activities.  They must also be a positive role model of behavior for the other scouts.  They all learned public speaking skills and about the democratic process.

  Closing Circle:  Creativity Lesson

Our new Guild Leader Skyler held the maraca as he led the closing circle for the first time.  Samantha led a fun discussion by asking the scouts some questions and they had to use their imagination and creativity to come up with possibilities.  One question she asked was, " How would a hat work if it were a laptop?"  The scouts came up with all kinds of ideas, some of them were a robot arm that would type for you, a mindreading hat that could know what you wanted it to do by telepathy, and a helmet with a visor that was a computer screen.  Samantha asked them what a gorilla would look like if it were magnified 1000 times and one scout said their hair would look like trees.  Then she asked what could you do with a gorilla that was shrunk down, and the responses were use it as a ball, teach it to massage, and train them to play minecraft.  This was a great closing circle and all the kids really enjoyed it!
