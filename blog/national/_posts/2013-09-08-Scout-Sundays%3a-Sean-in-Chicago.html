  Sean, age 7 from Guild 024, Oak Park (Chicago)


What kinds of things do you like to make?
I've only done it few times, but cap rockets.  It's where I mix vinegar and baking soda in a sealed container and then I just wait a few seconds and then boom! it just flies off.


What is your favorite project you have done?
Definitely electrolysis of water.  It's where water is separated into its component parts, because electricity breaks those bonds but you have to put in some sodium sulphate or some sodium chloride, sodium sulphate works better.  Sodium chloride isn't that hard to find, it's the same thing as salt!  But as chemists call it, table salt.
Link to video is here: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pQcVNihFvEE
