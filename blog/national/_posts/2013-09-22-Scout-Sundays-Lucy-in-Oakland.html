This week we are heading back to Oakland because Simon's sister was highly offended she wasn't interviewed as well.

Lucy, age 4.5

Sparks 001, Oakland CA

What kinds of things do you like to make?

Well, I like to sew and I like to use the hammer to make things. I made a treasure chest by myself.

(Lucy starts singing) I've got gadgets and gizmos a plenty! I've got whozits and whatits galore!

What is your favorite project you have done?

I made a cardboard dollhouse that is painted and has wallpaper and rugs inside. It's amazing.

What are you excited about learning in the future?

I would like to learn how to use the sewing machine.

Which badges are your favorites/most excited about?

I would like to do robotics so I can build a robot pony.

What do you enjoy about getting together with your Hacker Scouts community?

I like seeing my friends and making things I didn't know how to make.

If you could make anything-what is your dream project?

Oh man, I would make a time travel machine. Then I would go back to Ancient India to do some art and get a massage.

What is your favorite flavor of ice cream?

Strawberry!

Favorite video game?

Are you kidding? Skylanders. I am even better than my brothers. If they tell you I'm not, they are lying.
