Seattle Open Lab


March 24th, Noon - 2:00pm

Metrix Create:Space

623A Broadway East

Seattle, WA 98102

http://metrixcreatespace.com


Calling all Hacker Families:


Join us for our first Hacker Scout Open Lab at Metrix.  Come learn something new, make something, get help with your project and make new friends!


Soldering - Learn to Solder*  Workshop on basic soldering skill, using an electronic kit from SparkFun.  Good for kids 8 and above, or capable younger kids with close parental supervision.  Pierce will be leading the workshop using the instructional comic, “Soldering is Easy,” which you can download as a PDF here:  http://mightyohm.com/files/soldercomic/FullSolderComic_EN.pdf


Laser-Cut Bird House* - Put together a birdhouse made on the laser cutter, then paint and decorate it.*  We will have watercolor paint to stain the birdhouse, and some decorations on hand.  Good for all ages.  


Bookmaking (free!)

Make a mini single-sheet zine, or a small chapbook/artbook/blank notebook using saddle-stich binding.  We’ll have paper, and cover paper available, and some materials for making collage covers, or handmade paper covers.  Kids can draw picture, make a mini comic book, blank notebook, or art book.  Feel free to bring anything special you’d like to add to your book such as: photos, buttons, ribbon, printed text, tissue paper etc.  Good for all ages.  


Open Labs are free to attend, but we are asking for a suggested donation of $10/per kid to help cover the cost of the space.   Donations over $10 welcome, and will help cover expenses for kids who are unable to afford it.  


* these workshops will have a material fee TBA.  We’ll be keeping the price as low as we can.  It should be somewhere around $15.
