Curiosity Hacked Winter Camps 2016

CH Winter Camps are unique and extraordinary, maintaining the same level of learner centered mentorship and possibilities as our year-long programs. We keep the ratio small and the ideas big, presenting concepts and skills in a way that offers children the freedom to self-determine and make choices that stay true to their own vision. We also have expansive age ranges to intentionally encourage mixed age learning. We set the minimum age in anticipation of motor skills and understanding, and create more complex challenges and leadership opportunities for the older kids. If you feel your child is ready for a particular camp, or have questions regarding one of our camps, please contact us. We hope your child will join us!

Curiosity Hacked Thanksgiving Camp: Electronic Galaxy

November 21st - 23rd

Join us as we explore space and technology through astronomy, robotics, rocketry, electronic clothing and cosplay, and our favorite futuristic space themed media! Lots of projects to build skills while encouraging confidence and personal vision! This 3 day camp will be out of this world!

Ages 8+ ( Please note: depending on age spread, we may group kids together to differentiate according to age and abilities.)

9am-3pm, after care available until 5pm

Register: 11/21-11/23
$270 / After Care 3-5pm $100

Curiosity Hacked Winter Camp: Fantasy & Magic

December 19th - 23rd

Join us as we explore the awesome realms of Fantasy and Magic through Making. In our Guild project, kids will work both independently and together to gain knowledge and complete challenges, resulting in a mastery badge for 6 different skill areas. Options will include game design and play, weaponry and armor, plant knowledge and uses, chemistry, wands and magical artifacts, cosplay, and more! Lots of projects to build skills while encouraging confidence and personal vision! Each Friday will end with an exhibition of our work to share with family and friends. Kids attending both weeks will be offered new projects the second week that expand on our theme!

Ages 8+ ( Please note: depending on age spread, we may group kids together to differentiate according to age and abilities.)

9am-3pm, after care available until 5pm

Register: closed
$450 / After Care 3-5pm $100
